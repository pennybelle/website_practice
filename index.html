★ A Relaxed RP ★

Refined RP is a community of folks dedicated to having fun on DayZ. RR has around 70 mods working in sync to create seemingly endless ways to have fun and create stories. We are proud to say that many friendships have been started through this community, and many people consider this their home server! We listen to the community, we remove toxicity and corruption at the root, and we work hard to have a truly fun and unique community experience on Refined DayZ.

What do we offer specifically?

★ Custom Traders, Loot, Vehicles, Zombie & Animal spawns, etc.

★ Helicopters & Boats

★ Gold & silver [panning & mining for gold & silver]

★ Plenty of ways to make money and progress [Selling pelts/meat, fungi, mili loot, mining, lumber, hardware supplies, etc] with an emphasis on player trading.

★ Lots of community loved mods [Windstrides, FS, MMG, Mung, Mass, Cannabis, etc]

★ 6 KOS Zones & 2 Static Gas Zones [as well as dynamic gas strike zones]

★ Custom Military zones & tiers

★ Organic Economy & Cool rare loot

★ Easter eggs & hidden lore, as well as custom builds meant for player bases

★ Admin & Community Events [including occasional PVP events]
